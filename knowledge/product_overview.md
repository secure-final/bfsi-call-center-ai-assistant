# Product Overview – Sample (Illustrative)

*For RAG retrieval. Details are indicative.*

## Personal Loan

- **Purpose**: Personal use, medical, travel, debt consolidation, etc.
- **Eligibility**: Salaried and self-employed; age and income as per product.
- **Documents**: ID, address, income proof, bank statements.
- **Disbursal**: To borrower’s account; timeline as per verification.

## Home Loan

- **Purpose**: Purchase, construction, or renovation of residential property.
- **Security**: Mortgage of property.
- **Tenure**: Long tenure; rate type (fixed/floating) as per product.

## Education Loan

- **Purpose**: Approved courses in India or abroad.
- **Margin**: As per policy; rest financed.
- **Moratorium**: Possible for course period + grace; interest may be serviced or capitalised as per terms.

## General

- All products are subject to eligibility and documentation.
- Rates and charges are as per prevailing policy and loan agreement.
- For current rates and exact terms, refer to our website or branch.
