## Late Payment (EMI)

- **Late payment fee**: As per loan agreement; typically a fixed amount or percentage of overdue EMI.
- **Bounce charges**: Applicable if auto-debit or cheque fails; amount as per tariff.
- **Impact**: Late payments may be reported to bureaus and can affect credit score.

*Exact charges are in your loan agreement and our tariff.*

## Minimum Balance (Savings Account)

- **Non-maintenance charges**: As per account type and tariff.
- **Zero-balance accounts**: No minimum balance requirement for eligible products.

*Check our tariff and your account type.*

## Cheque and Stop Payment

- **Stop payment**: Charge as per tariff per request.
- **Bounce (insufficient funds)**: Charges as per tariff.

*Tariff is available on our website and at branches.*

## Prepayment / Foreclosure Charges

- **Personal loan**: Often nil or minimal after lock-in; refer to agreement.
- **Home loan**: May have prepayment charges during lock-in; floating rate loans may have lower or nil charges as per policy.

*Your loan agreement specifies exact terms.*
